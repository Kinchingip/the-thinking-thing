## Project overview

This wiki serves as the internal documentation system for the Harrow facility's primary research project, designated **SUBSTRATE**. Access is restricted to the twelve members of the core research team.

Project SUBSTRATE concerns the development and practical application of a coherent electromagnetic broadcast capable of separating the bioelectric signature of conscious cognition from its biological substrate and transcribing it to a compatible digital architecture. The team has achieved successful transcription in controlled conditions.
